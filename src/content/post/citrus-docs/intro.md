## Welcome to MaiPDF

MaiPDF is a cutting-edge platform designed to revolutionize how you share, protect, and track PDF documents. Whether you're a business professional, educator, or content creator, MaiPDF provides the tools you need to maintain control over your documents while providing seamless access to authorized users.

## About MaiPDF Platform

MaiPDF transforms traditional PDF sharing by adding layers of security, control, and analytics that were previously unavailable. Our platform addresses the common challenges of document distribution:

- **Secure Link Generation**: Create password-protected, time-limited links for your PDFs
- **Access Control**: Set reading limits, time restrictions, and user permissions
- **Copy Protection**: Prevent unauthorized copying, printing, and screenshots
- **Real-time Analytics**: Track who accesses your documents and when
- **User-friendly Interface**: Intuitive dashboard for managing all your documents
